Preliminary English test(PET)- A2 Certificate

The Preliminary English Test (PET) - A2, also known as Cambridge English A2 Key (KET), is an internationally recognized English language qualification provided by Cambridge Assessment English. This certification demonstrates proficiency in basic English communication skills, essential for everyday conversations and academic or professional growth.

Key Details:

  • Understanding and using basic phrases and expressions
  • Writing short messages, emails, and simple notes
  • Reading basic texts and instructions
  • Listening and understanding slow, clear speech
  • Speaking and introducing oneself in conversations
